    • Mission

    VwithU is here to help

    For a meritorious student, the economic struggle should not be a reason to be denied an education. VwithU is centered around this very idea. By our scholarship program, we provide financial-aid to bright students from underprivileged backgrounds.

    • Challenge

      The underprivileged community does not have access to quality higher education, and we target the disadvantaged community.

    • Opportunity

      The objective of VwithU is creating educated and skilled human resources out of the underprivileged children. We aim to educate the disadvantaged children from every nook and corner of India by providing them access to higher education so that they can work for the betterment of society and break the vicious cycle of poverty.

    • Our Approach

      "Free-of-Cost" higher education to the underprivileged children, by our scholarship program, we provide financial-aid to bright students from underprivileged backgrounds.
